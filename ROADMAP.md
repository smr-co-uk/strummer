# Version 2

## Drums
Support optional drum pattern sections using named drum lanes mapped to General MIDI 
percussion notes. Something like this
drums:
K: X--- X--- X--- X---
S: ---- X--- ---- X---
H: X-X- X-X- X-X- X-X-

D- DU D- DU | DU D- D- DU | D- DU D- DU | DU D- D- DU


subdivision: 8

drums:
K: X- -- X- --
S: -- X- -- X-
H: XX XX XX XX

Tempo: 90
Time: 4/4

HH|x-x-x-x-x-x-x-x-|
SD|----o-------o---|
BD|o-------o-o-----|

HH|x-x-x-x-x-x-x-x-|
SD|----o-------o---|
BD|o-------o-o-----|
   1 & 2 & 3 & 4 &
C |----------------|
HH|xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx|
SD|----o-------o---|
BD|o-------oo------|
   1e&a2e&a3e&a4e&a

HH|X-x-x-X-x-x-x-x-|
SD|----g-------O---|
BD|O-------o-o-----|
* X = accented hit
* x = normal hit
* O = accented drum hit
* o = normal drum hit
* g = ghost note

* HH = Hi-hat
* SD = Snare drum
* BD = Bass (kick) drum
* x = Cymbal hit
* o = Drum hit
